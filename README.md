# HITV-Content-Performance-Analysis-from-2003-to-2022
This report analyzes how the HITV Content Performance Dashboard performed between 2003 and 2022, highlighting content growth trends, genre distribution, rating patterns, production output, and key contributors across the HITV platform

## Introduction
The goal of this analysis is to understand how HITV content evolved over time, identify dominant genres, evaluate rating variations, and highlight key contributors such as directors, actors, and production companies.

### Key Focus Areas
- Most productive release year
- Highest-rated dramas
- Dominant genres and tags
- Key production networks and talent

These insights support decision-making in content acquisition, production investment, and audience engagement strategies.

## Dataset Description
The dataset includes HITV drama records from 2003 to 2022 with the following features:

- Title Name
- Rating
- Genre
- Release Year
- Director
- Screenwriter
- Actor
- Network
- Production Company
- Content Tag

Some entries include multiple genres and contributors.

## Data Preprocessing
- Removed duplicate titles
- Reviewed missing values
- Standardized genre categories
- Ordered release years chronologically
- Assigned dominant genres for multi-genre entries
- Calculated KPIs such as average rating and total dramas

### Variables
- Dependent: Average Rating, Number of Dramas, Episode Count
- Independent: Genre, Year, Actor, Director, Network, Production Company

## Pre-Analysis Insights
- Total dramas: 250
- Average rating: 8.56
- Romance dominates genre distribution
- Content production increased after 2015
- Ratings remained consistently high
- Strong female lead narratives are common

## In-Analysis Findings

### Key Metrics
- Top Release Year: 2021
- Highest Rated Drama: Move to Heaven
- Highest Episode Count: 133
- Most Common Genre: Romance
- Top Content Tag: Strong Female Lead
- Top Network: tvN
- Top Production Company: Studio Dragon

### Genre Insights
- Romance leads significantly
- Comedy and Mystery follow
- Thriller and Action are moderate
- Historical and Political are niche

### Ratings Insights
- Most genres have ratings above 8.3
- Medical, Supernatural, and Drama rank highest

### People & Production
- Top Director: Kim Won Suk
- Top Screenwriter: Kim Young H
- Top Actor: Song Joong Ki
- Production is concentrated among few networks and studios

### Content Growth
- Slow growth before 2010
- Gradual increase (2010–2016)
- Rapid expansion from 2017
- Peak between 2019–2022
